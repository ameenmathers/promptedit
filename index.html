FAQ

Questions we get a lot.

Do I own what I generate?

Yes. Every output on a paid plan is licensed for commercial use, including client work, ads, and broadcast. Starter outputs are watermarked and for testing only.

How do credits work across models?

One credit pool, every model. A 5 second Veo clip is about 40 credits. A Nano Banana image is about 3. We show the cost before you click Generate, and you only pay for outputs you keep.

Do the extensions need a separate license?

No. The Premiere and DaVinci panels ship with Creator and Studio. Sign in with your PromptEdit account and your credits, history and library are right there.

What happens to unused credits?

They roll over for up to 12 months on Creator, indefinitely on Studio. We don't expire credits while your subscription is active.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. One click in your dashboard, no email gymnastics. Within 30 days, full refund on request.

How fast do new models get added?

Usually within a week of public release. Veo 3 landed on PromptEdit four days after launch.
